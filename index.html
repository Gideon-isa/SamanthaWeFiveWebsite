We Five

Founded in 2021 by Samantha; a former instructor in The prestigious Royal Stockholm Philharmonic Orchestra in Sweden. She has worked with notable Composers of beautiful music pieces with some of the Instructors of theRoyal Stockholm Philharmonic Orchestra.

The development of the We Five Orchestra Group began with the idea Smantha had to help the young generation of Swedish national who have lost the pleasure in classical music.

The We Five Orchestra is one now of the most prominet and leading orchestras in the nation with its commitment to artistic excellence and its ambitious approaches to music performance and collaboration. The orchestra is made up of best instrumentalist in their fields to dazzles everyone who have hears for quality Classical Music.

Its landmark achievement was the performance, of Mozart’s Opera “the Magic Flute” in the original German language, to wide public acclaim. There were two additional performances of the Opera due to public demand. Encouraged by this development, Smantha and her group have resolved to build on the orchestra’s remarkable success by the recent engagement of a it's supporters.

The We Five is grateful to the forward-thinking businesses and foundations that value the role of the arts in making our group strong and vibrant. Their generous support fuels dazzling musical experiences that enrich, inspire, and serve people throughout Sweden, across the country and around the world.

The We Five makes exclusive performances for pre-booked reservations, at Weddings, AGMs, Conferences, and exclusive private functions.
